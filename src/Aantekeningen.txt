Start
1) Get Files: we halen de files op en stoppen deze in een vector.
resultaat [a.text], [b.text]

2) Read files
string content = readFile(file);
Dit wordt voor: 
a.txt: "Hello world\nHello AI\n"
b.txt: "Hello world AI\nBye world\n"

3) Text Processing (Tokenize)
tokenize(content)
a.txt wordt: ["hello", "world", "hello", "ai"]
b.txt wordt: ["hello", "world", "ai", "bye", "world"]

4) Frequency map
a.txt:
buildFrequencyMap(words)
hello → 2
world → 1
ai → 1

b.txt:
hello → 1
world → 2
ai → 1
bye → 1

5) Inverted Index(globaal systeem)
We combineren alles (Soort bucketsoort)
word → set<files>
hello → {a.txt, b.txt}
world → {a.txt, b.txt}
ai    → {a.txt, b.txt}
bye   → {b.txt}

6) MultiSearch(Intersection)
Als query: "hello bye"
hello → {a.txt, b.txt}
Beide komen voor in b.txt: Intersection
bye   → {b.txt}
